# POCKETFYT Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Brand Name:** POCKETFYT  
**Tagline:** Prices that fit your pocket

**Brand Personality:** Bright, friendly, bold, simple, reliable. Everything must feel affordable, accessible and fast.

## Color System

- **Primary Orange:** `#FF6B00` - Headers, buttons, and CTA areas
- **Secondary Dark Gray:** `#333333` - Card outlines and dashboards  
- **Accent Green:** `#3CCF4E` - Cash prices and verification badges only
- **Text White:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Background Light Cream:** `#FFF6EB`

## Typography

- Clean sans-serif font family throughout
- Clear hierarchy with bold headings
- Readable body text on cream background

## Layout Principles

- **Simple layouts, no clutter**
- **Rounded corners** for all cards and buttons
- Generous whitespace for breathing room
- Mobile-first responsive approach
- Location badges prominently displayed on product cards

## Component Specifications

### Homepage Structure

**Header:**
- Logo positioned left
- Login button positioned right
- Clean horizontal layout

**Hero Section:**
- Title: "Find the best price that fits your pocket"
- Subtitle: "Compare prices across verified sellers in your area"
- Primary CTA: "Get Started" button in orange (#FF6B00)
- Use a hero image showing marketplace/shopping/products

**Popular Items Preview:**
Display grid of popular products with prices:
- Beans ₦1500
- Rice ₦1500  
- Bread ₦1500
- Pizza ₦1500

**Wholesale Preview Section:**
Show bulk pricing examples:
- 50 bags Beans ₦500000
- 20 bags Rice ₦180000
- 10 crates Drinks ₦55000

**Footer:**
- Contact information
- Legal links

### Role Selection Screen

After "Get Started", display role options:
- Sign up as Buyer
- Sign up as Seller  
- Sign up as Wholesaler
- Login as Buyer
- Login as Seller
- Login as Wholesaler

### Dashboard Layouts

**Buyer Dashboard:**
- Location-filtered product feed (nearest sellers first)
- Popular products section
- Buy with cash option (green accent)
- Price comparison view
- Saved items
- Notifications panel

**Seller Dashboard:**
- Upload product form
- Inventory management grid
- Real-time price editing interface
- Buyer interactions
- Analytics charts

**Wholesaler Dashboard:**
- Bulk upload interface
- Tier pricing builder
- Inventory overview
- Bulk buyer connections
- Logistics options

### Product Cards

**Retail Product Card:**
- Product image (square ratio)
- Product name
- Price (prominent)
- Location badge showing distance
- "Compare Prices" button
- Shipping cost estimate when distance is high

**Wholesale Product Card:**
- Product image
- Large quantity display
- Tier pricing table
- Delivery options
- National availability (no location filter)

### Product Upload Form

- Image upload with real-time preview
- Product name input
- Category dropdown
- Retail price field
- Wholesale price field (optional)
- Tier price builder for wholesalers
- Stock quantity input
- Auto-detect location display

### Interactive Elements

**Buttons:**
Primary orange background (#FF6B00), white text, rounded corners

**Button Texts:**
- Get Started
- Sign up as [Role]
- Login
- Upload Product
- Compare Prices
- Buy with Cash (with green accent)
- Add Stock
- Save Item
- Publish Now

**Location Display:**
- Distance badges on product cards
- City/state labels
- Proximity indicators

## Spacing & Visual Hierarchy

- Consistent padding within cards
- Clear separation between sections
- Bold typography for headings
- Price information always prominent

## Images

**Hero Section:** Full-width background image showing marketplace, shopping, or products in motion - vibrant and inviting

**Product Cards:** Square product photos with consistent sizing

**Dashboard Graphics:** Simple iconography for analytics and stats

## Key UX Patterns

- **Location-first matching** for retail products (show nearest sellers)
- Distance badges visible on all retail product cards
- Override location filter when buyer selects wholesale/bulk quantities
- Green accent exclusively for cash-related features and verification
- Real-time price updates for sellers
- Tier pricing clearly displayed for wholesale products
- Shipping cost transparency when distance increases
